What is Pest Control
Pest control involves more than simply identifying a pest and using a control tactic. The treatment site, whether it is outdoors or indoors, usually contains other living organisms (such as people, animals, and plants) and nonliving surroundings (such as air, water, structures, objects, and surfaces).
